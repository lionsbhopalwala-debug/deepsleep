Supplements FAQ

1. Will these supplements make me groggy in the morning? ↓

Unlike pharmaceutical sedatives, natural compounds like **L-Theanine** have a short half-life. They help you fall asleep without interfering with your morning "Cortisol Awakening Response," leaving you fresh at sunrise.

2. Is Valerian Root better than Melatonin? ↓

They serve different purposes. Melatonin sets the "clock" (Timing), while **Valerian Root** acts on GABA receptors to reduce "latency" (the time it takes to fall asleep).

3. Can I take these every single night? ↓

Amino acids like L-Theanine are safe for daily use. However, we recommend cycling adaptogens like **Ashwagandha** (5 days on, 2 days off) to maintain receptor sensitivity.

4. Do these supplements help with REM or Deep Sleep? ↓

Most mineral-based stacks (Magnesium/Zinc) enhance **Slow-Wave Deep Sleep**, whereas precursors like 5-HTP or Tryptophan can influence the intensity of **REM (Dreaming) Sleep**.

5. What is the "Golden Window" for supplementation? ↓

To align with your metabolic rate, ingest your stack **30 to 45 minutes** before your scheduled "DeepSleep Pro" bedtime. This ensures peak concentration during the first 90-minute cycle.
