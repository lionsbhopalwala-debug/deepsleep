Scientific Precision: The 90-Minute Rule

Every night, your brain moves through several sleep cycles. A complete cycle typically lasts 90 minutes. DeepSleep Pro calculates the exact moment your cycle ends, allowing you to wake up naturally refreshed.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How many hours of sleep do I really need?

5 cycles (7.5 hours) or 6 cycles (9 hours) are the gold standards for cognitive recovery.

2. Can a 20-minute nap really help?

Yes! A "Power Nap" provides a boost in alertness without entering deep sleep.
