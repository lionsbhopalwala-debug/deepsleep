Frequently Asked Questions

Why 9 Sleep Cycles?

Most people need 5-6 cycles. However, some athletes and high-performance individuals require up to 9 cycles for physical recovery. Over-sleeping beyond this can cause fatigue.

Is Caffeine that bad?

Caffeine blocks Adenosine, the chemical that makes you feel sleepy. It can reduce the quality of Deep REM sleep, even if you manage to fall asleep easily.
