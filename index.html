"Why 5 or 6 Cycles?"

Scientific studies show that for 95% of healthy adults, 7.5 hours (5 cycles) or 9 hours (6 cycles) is the optimum duration for cognitive restoration and metabolic health. Going beyond 9 cycles (Over-sleeping) is often linked to increased risk of chronic fatigue.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I survive on 4 cycles?

While 6 hours (4 cycles) can be done occasionally, long-term sleep debt leads to decreased immunity and high cortisol levels.

How does caffeine affect this calculator?

Caffeine blocks Adenosine receptors. Even if you sleep, your brain may skip deep REM stages. Our tool adjusts the recommendation label based on your intake.
