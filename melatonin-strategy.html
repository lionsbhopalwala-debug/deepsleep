Mastering the "Vampire Hormone" and the "Master Mineral" for deep neural recovery.

The Timing Hormone

Melatonin koi neend ki goli (sedative) nahi hai. Ye sirf ek timing signal hai jo brain ko batata hai ke kab 90-minute sleep cycles shuru karne hain. Hamari strategy ye hai ke isay sirf tab use kiya jaye jab aapka "Internal Clock" (Circadian Rhythm) kharab ho chuka ho.

Precision Dosage Protocol

Zyaada log ghalti ye karte hain ke wo 5mg ya 10mg melatonin lete hain. High-performance bio-hacking mein sirf 0.3mg to 3mg ki dose recommend ki jati hai. Magnesium ke liye, 200-400mg of Magnesium Glycinate sone se 1 ghanta pehle lene se muscles relax ho jate hain aur deep sleep ki quality behtar hoti hai.

Strategy FAQ

1. Kya Melatonin aur Magnesium saath le sakte hain? ↓

Ji haan! Ye ek zabardast combination hai. Magnesium aapki body ko "Physical relaxation" deta hai, jabke Melatonin brain ko "Sleep signal" deta hai. Dono mil kar neend ki quality behtar banate hain.

2. Kaunsa Magnesium neend ke liye best hai? ↓

Magnesium Glycinate sab se behtar hai. Is se pet kharab nahi hota aur ye brain ko relax karne mein sab se zyada effective hai.

3. Kya Melatonin ki aadat par jati hai? ↓

Technically nahi, lekin agar aap ise rozana use karenge toh brain natural melatonin banana kam kar sakta hai. Ise hamesha "short-term tool" ke taur par use karein.

4. Magnesium lene ka sahi waqt kya hai? ↓

Bedtime se takriban 60 minutes pehle. Is se aapke muscles aur nerves ko relax hone ka poora waqt milta hai.

5. Melatonin se subha bhojpan (Grogginess) kyun hoti hai? ↓

Ye aksar tab hota hai jab aap Dose bohat zyada lete hain (5mg+). Kam dose (1mg) lene se aap subha bilkul fresh uthte hain.
